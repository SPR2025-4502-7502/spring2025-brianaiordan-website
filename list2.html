7 best spots for students to visit on MU's campus

Looking for the best spots to visit on the University of Missouri's campus? Here are seven must-visit places that MU students should know about.

MU Student Center

901 Rollins St.
Columbia, MO
65211
(573) 882-6310

Visit the MU Student Center's website

The MU Student Center — a central hub in the heart of campus — offers numerous amenities, including meeting rooms, food places and the Mizzou Store. It is the perfect gathering place on campus and has a lively, welcoming environment.

Mizzou Rec

1000 Rollins St.
Columbia, MO
65211
(573) 882-0369

Visit Mizzou Rec's website

MU’s recreation center — one of the best recreation centers in the country — is a fitness facility with a multitude of amenities from fitness equipment to recreational sports to a rock-climbing wall to an indoor pool.

The Quad

410 S. Sixth St.
Columbia, MO
65211
(573) 882-2121

Visit The Quad's website

The Quad is the home of MU’s iconic columns and Jesse Hall among other historic buildings. Students can enjoy lounging in the grass on a warm, sunny day. The Quad is also the location of several student activities throughout the year.

Memorial Student Union

518 Hitt St.
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 882-6310

Visit Memorial Student Union's website

MU’s Memorial Student Union is a historic building and common central campus gathering spot for student organizations. It is also the home of Starbucks and Wheatstone Bistro, which are part of MU’s dining services. The building stands out for its architecture and is an important social and cultural place at MU.

Ellis Library

520 S. Ninth St.
Columbia, MO
65211
(573) 882-4701

Visit Ellis Library's website

Ellis Library is the largest and most extensive library on MU’s campus. The library is a vital academic resource and a quiet, supportive study spot. In addition to checking out books, at Ellis, librarians offer research support and specialized services.

Faurot Field

600 E. Stadium Blvd.
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 882-6501

Visit Faurot Field's website

Faurot Field is MU’s stadium and the home of the Mizzou Tigers football team. The stadium opened in 1926 and can seat over 60,000 people. The stadium provides an electric atmosphere and a venue for Tiger fans to come together.

Peace Park

401 S. Ninth St.
Columbia, MO
65201
(573) 884-6307

Visit Peace Park's website

McAlester Park, better known as Peace Park, is the oldest undisturbed land on MU’s campus. It provides a lush, beautiful space for students to relax and take a break surrounded by nature.
